Ibrahim Kipkemboi Hussein

Personal Information

Date of Birth: June 3, 1958

Place of Birth: Kapsabet, Rift Valley, Kenya

Nationality: Kenyan

Profession: Retired Long-Distance Runner; Athletics Administrator; Businessman

Career Highlights

  • Boston Marathon Champion: 1988, 1991, 1992
  • New York City Marathon Winner: 1987
  • Olympic Participation: 1988 (Seoul), 1992 (Barcelona)
  • Personal Best Marathon Time: 2:08:14 (Boston, 1992)

Education

  • St. Patrick's High School, Iten, Kenya
  • University of New Mexico, Albuquerque, USA

Professional Experience

  • Regional Director for World Athletics (English-speaking Africa)
  • Owner of Amedo Center, Eldoret, Kenya
  • Operator of a training camp for young Kenyan athletes
  • Chairman of Athletics Kenya North Rift branch

Honors and Recognitions

  • Inducted into the New York Road Runners Hall of Fame (2021)
  • First African to win both the Boston and New York City Marathons
  • Achieved the closest finish in Boston Marathon history at the time (1988)
  • Recognized in the New Mexico Hall of Fame

Personal Life

  • Married to Zainab Ibrahim
  • Children: Hussein, Farida, Hadija, Jamal, Warda Ibrahim
  • Resides in Eldoret, Kenya
